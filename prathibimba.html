CAMPUS CLUB HUB

PRATHIBIMBA

The official art club of the college. The club is a lively place for students who love to be creative. It's where artists can express themselves, try out different art techniques, and get better at what they do. The club's main goals are to support artistic talent, encourage new ideas, and let members show their art at exhibitions and events in the college. It's not just about becoming better artists; it's also about being part of a community, feeling unique, and understanding art better. This club adds a colorful and enriching part to college life, making it more exciting for those who love art.
