CAMPUS CLUB HUB

TATVA

TATVA, which is the drama club of the college, is a vibrant hub of creativity, where students passionately explore the art of acting, directing, and production. With a diverse mix of talents, the club stages compelling performances that captivate audiences. It serves as a platform for budding playwrights, actors, and behind-the-scenes enthusiasts to collaborate and hone their skills. Weekly rehearsals buzz with energy as members immerse themselves in character development and script analysis. The drama club also organizes workshops, inviting professionals to share insights and techniques. Beyond the spotlight, the camaraderie among members fosters a supportive community, making the drama club an integral and enriching part of college life.
